# Screenshot Plan

Store listings need real screenshots captured from the actual extension. **Do not use mockups, placeholders, or fabricated images.** Capture these from a real session, then remove or blur any sensitive text before uploading.

## Store size guidance

- **Chrome Web Store:** screenshots 1280x800 or 640x400 (1280x800 recommended). At least one is required; up to five are allowed.
- **Microsoft Edge Add-ons:** screenshots up to 1280x800 (minimum 640x400). At least one is required.

Capture at 1280x800 so the same images can be reused for both stores.

## Required screenshots

1. **Clean setup screen** - the options/settings page with the AI chat URL field and the personalization fields visible (use neutral, non-sensitive example text).
2. **Three-mode toolbar** - an editable field with text selected, showing the Quick / Normal / Formal toolbar.
3. **Suggestion preview** - the panel showing a returned AI suggestion with the Apply and Cancel buttons.
4. **Recovery card** - one of the recovery states:
   - "Set up your AI chat" (missing URL), or
   - "Could not reach your AI chat" (unreachable chat).
5. **Settings screen** - the full options page, including the personalization fields (About Me, Global Prompt, Avoid These Things) and the "Save and open AI chat" button.

## Optional extra

- A short before/after example showing a rough sentence rewritten in Normal mode (use invented, non-sensitive text).

## Capture rules

- Use **invented, non-sensitive sample text** only. Never show real private messages, names, emails, passwords, tokens, or AI conversation URLs.
- Blur or crop the browser address bar if it shows a real conversation URL.
- Keep the new Re-Phraser icon visible in the toolbar where relevant.
- Save final images under `docs/assets/screenshots/` (create the folder) before submission. They are for the store listing and are not shipped inside the extension ZIP.
